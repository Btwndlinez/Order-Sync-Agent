Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 11, 2026

Information We Collect

Order Sync Agent is designed with privacy-first principles. We only collect information that is essential for providing our AI-powered checkout generation service.

Essential Data Only

We use LocalStorage for essential functionality only - no cookie consent banner required at launch.

  • Messenger Conversation Analysis: We analyze message content locally in your browser to detect purchase intent and match products from your catalog.
  • Authentication Tokens: Secure Stripe tokens are stored in LocalStorage for checkout generation functionality.
  • Usage Statistics: Limited analytics for billing and subscription management (links generated, plan type).
  • Product Catalog: Your product data is processed securely in Supabase for AI matching - no personal data stored.

How We Use Your Information

  • Generate personalized product recommendations based on conversation content
  • Create accurate Stripe checkout links for products you select
  • Manage subscription and usage limits for billing purposes
  • Improve AI matching algorithms and service performance

Data Storage & Security

Local Storage (Privacy-First)

Essential Only: All conversation data is stored locally in your browser using LocalStorage. We do not use cookies that require consent banners.

Encrypted: Data is encrypted at rest and only accessible by the extension.

Cloud Storage (Supabase)

Secure: Product catalog and authentication data is stored securely in Supabase with encryption in transit and at rest.

Minimal: Only essential business data (product info, subscription status) - no conversation content.

PCI Compliant: All payment processing is handled by Stripe - we never store credit card information.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We only share data as necessary to:

  • Payment Processing: Process payments through Stripe's secure platform
  • Service Operation: Store data securely in Supabase for service functionality
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with applicable laws and regulations

Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your data at any time
  • Correction: Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Export: Export your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out: Disable analytics and data collection from extension settings

Data Retention

  • Local Data: Stored in LocalStorage until you uninstall the extension or clear browser data.
  • Account Data: Retained until you request deletion. Usage logs are kept for 12 months for billing and analytics.
  • Right to Delete: You can delete your account and all associated data at any time.
